Dana's Deli, located on 3900 Biltmore Avenue in Oakdale, is a family owned deli since 1940 when Daniel McDuff founded it in Oakdale, NY. He named the deli after his daughter, Dana. The deli has been handed down from generation to generation, preserving the familty tradition of delicious food and terrific service with a smile! Today, Daniel's great grandson, who is also called Dana, runs the place.

Dana's Deli is open all year round except for Christmas and Thanksgiving! We are open from 6AM to 7PM from Monday to Friday and from 7AM to 1PM on Saturdays and Sundays! If you would like to order from our take out only deli, our phone number is (631) 555-9772! Come on down and try a piece of our sandwich heaven!
